Graphic Design as a Video Game (Persona 5)

I’ve always wondered what my life as a graphic design student would be like if it were a video game. This video shows me going to the URBN Center (Drexel’s Design Building) and getting some design work done.

The UI Design was inspired by Persona 5. I made the assets in Sketch, animated the UI in After Effects, and assembled everything together in Premiere Pro. Some of the UI sounds were taken from Persona 5 and Ace Attorney, while environmental sounds (door opening, id beep, etc.) were recorded on my phone.

This project gave me a chance to finally bring an idea I’ve had for a long time to life. It gave me valuable experience in directing a cameraman as well.

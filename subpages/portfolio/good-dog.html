Good Dog Branding

For this project, I was assigned the name of a fictional company. I had to develop a unique brand identity for the company, develop four different logos, and apply one of them to a design.

I envisioned Good Dog as a dog training company dedicated to training service dogs.

I designed a series of labels for a limited run of “Good Dog” branded dog food that would help spread awareness of the brand. I carefully considered the FDA guidelines for packaging design as well as how the product would look on store shelves when crafting the design.

Jagat Magazine

Jagat Magazine is a news magazine targeted towards the younger generation of Indians in their twenties and thirties. It aims to educate them on the social issues going on in their countries. The magazine is in Hindi and English.

This issue’s cover story talks about India’s caste system. It talks about Suraj Yengde, a man born into the lowest caste, the Untouchables. Even though Suraj moved to America, the stigma and isolation of being associated with the Untouchables followed him. I focused my creative direction on the concept of a man being split between two countries. This is something I could relate to a lot, since I feel like I’m pulled between my Indian heritage and my American upbringing.

This was one of the most personal projects I’ve ever done. I leaned into my Indian heritage and created something unique to me.

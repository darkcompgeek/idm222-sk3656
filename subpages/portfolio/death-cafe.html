Death Cafe London Underground Advertisements

In Summer of 2019, I studied abroad in London. As part of my work there, I was assigned a place in London to explore, and I had to design a piece around it. I was assigned the Brompton Cemetery.

During my stay in London, I developed a fascination for London’s transportation system. I wanted to incorporate this newfound interest into my final design, so I developed a series of advertisements for an event at the Brompton Cemetery. These ads would be displayed throughout the London Underground.

I used a green color scheme to reflect the lush greenery found at the cemetery. I went with a very formal typographic direction match the “serious” nature of the cemetery.

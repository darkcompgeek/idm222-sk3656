Spice Kit Packaging

For my packaging class, I developed and designed the packaging for an Indian Spice Kit targeted towards adults living in the United States who are newcomers to Indian cuisine and want to make authentic Indian dishes. This spice kit contains the 6 essential spices used in Indian cuisine.

I created line drawings of the plants that the spices are picked from and used them as elements throughout the design.

I created 3D Model of the product in Blender and rendered mockups of the final packaging from different angles to showcase the product in a three-dimensional setting.

Downton Abbey Title Sequence

For this project, we had to design and animate the title sequence for a TV Show of our choice. I chose Downton Abbey because I am fascinated by the elegance and lavishness of British aristocratic culture.

Downton Abbey is a show about the lives of the rich, upscale family living there, the poorer servants keeping the house running, and how those two lives intertwine. I wanted to represent these interactions in an abstract way, so I hand-drew a series of patterns to represent the two lives. The more elegant, vibrant patterns represent the rich, upscale family, while the more drab, simpler patterns represent the servants.

I animated this project in After Effects.

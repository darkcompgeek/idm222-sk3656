Hi! I'm Sai.

As long as I can remember, I've had a passion for design and storytelling. I grew up sketching User Interface Designs in class, and I wrote a lot of short stories of short stories as a kid.

Eventually, this continued interest led me to Drexel, where the rigorous Graphic Design Program helped me push my skills even further. To this day, I apply the same passion for design and storytelling in my work.

I have experience in many areas of design, such as print, branding, and web. My ambition and dedication to quality drives me as I work. If I don't know a piece of software, I rigorously teach it to myself.

When I'm not designing, I love to read, explore techology, and explore Philly.
